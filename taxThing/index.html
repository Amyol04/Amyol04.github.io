Monthly Revenue Tax Calculator


Key Features
  • Step-by-Step Input: Enter your revenue one month at a time using an easy-to-follow modal dialog.
  • Accurate Tax Calculation: Automatically calculates your tax based on a fixed rate of 12.5% for each month.
  • Detailed Summary: Get a comprehensive overview of your tax information, including the highest, lowest, and average taxes.
  • Important Reminder: Receive a timely alert to ensure you pay your taxes before the January 31st deadline.
How It Works
  • Enter Your Revenue: Use the input fields to enter your monthly revenue. The modal dialog guides you through each month.
  • Automatic Calculations: The application calculates the tax for each month in real-time.
  • View Results: Once all data is entered, view your tax summary, including key figures and reminders.
